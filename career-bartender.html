2020 – 2023

Bartender & Floor Manager

Pretoria, South Africa  ·  Square Time Café

During my time at Square Time Café, I initially worked as a bartender before being promoted to a part-time shift supervisor. In this role I was responsible not only for bar service but also for a range of operational duties that supported the smooth running of the venue.

My responsibilities included closing procedures, cash reconciliation, coordinating staff clock-outs, managing inventory, and restocking the bar and refrigeration areas. These tasks required a high level of trust and reliability, as I was often responsible for securing the venue and ensuring the business closed correctly at the end of the evening.

Working in a busy bar environment also required strong communication, conflict management, and customer service skills. The role involved interacting with a wide range of customers, including handling challenging situations calmly and professionally when guests became disruptive or difficult. Through this experience I developed much greater confidence, learning how to stand my ground, communicate clearly, and resolve situations diplomatically.

I also took pride in delivering attentive service to regular customers and maintaining a high standard of work during busy shifts. Over time I became known for my reliability and work ethic, building strong relationships with both colleagues and regular patrons.

Although the role began as a simple service job, it became an important personal and professional learning experience. It helped me develop confidence, responsibility, and strong interpersonal skills, all of which have continued to benefit me in my later work and leadership roles.
